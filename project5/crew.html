Luffy's Crew

Monkey D. Luffy

Captain

The captain of the Straw Hat Pirates, Luffy is a fearless leader with a dream to become the Pirate King. His Gomu Gomu no Mi powers make him a rubber man with incredible strength and resilience.

Roronoa Zoro

Swordsman

Zoro is a master swordsman who wields three swords with unmatched skill. He dreams of becoming the world's greatest swordsman and is fiercely loyal to Luffy.

Nami

Navigator

A skilled navigator and cartographer, Nami dreams of mapping the entire world. She uses her intelligence and weather-controlling weapon, the Clima-Tact, to protect her crew.

Usopp

Sniper

Known for his sharp shooting and creativity, Usopp dreams of becoming a brave warrior of the sea. He is the Straw Hat Pirates' comedic relief and a loyal friend.

Sanji

Chef

Sanji is the crew's chef, known for his culinary mastery and powerful kicks. His dream is to find the All Blue, a legendary sea where all the fish of the world gather.

Tony Tony Chopper

Doctor

A reindeer with the ability to transform into a human, Chopper is the crew's doctor. He dreams of becoming a doctor who can cure any disease.
