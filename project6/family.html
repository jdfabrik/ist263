Luffy's Family

Monkey D. Garp

Grandfather

Luffy's grandfather, a Marine hero known as "Garp the Fist," has an unparalleled sense of justice and strength. Despite his ties to the Marines, Garp shares a deep bond with Luffy and trained him during his childhood.

Monkey D. Dragon

Father

The mysterious leader of the Revolutionary Army, Dragon is determined to overthrow the World Government and liberate oppressed nations. Although distant, he deeply cares for Luffy and keeps track of his son's adventures.

Portgas D. Ace

Adopted Brother

Luffy's older brother and the son of Gol D. Roger, Ace wielded the powers of the Mera Mera no Mi, a fire-based Devil Fruit. He was fiercely protective of Luffy and shared many adventures during their youth.

Sabo

Adopted Brother

The Chief of Staff of the Revolutionary Army, Sabo is a skilled fighter and a loyal friend to Luffy. He is known for his passionate drive to fight oppression and his mastery of the Mera Mera no Mi.
